# Belleza Sales Analysis Report
## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data from Belleza, a skincare and cosmetics company. The dataset was cleaned, standardized, and visualized using Power BI to uncover key business insights, sales trends, product performance, and regional distribution patterns over a six-month period.

## Key Insights
1. Product Performance
o The least selling product is *Charcoal Face Wash*, which recorded **39%** lower sales compared to the best-selling product, *Tea Tree Moisturizer*.
o *Body Butter Cream* and *Rose Water Toner* generated high sales revenue with fewer boxes shipped, indicating strong profitability despite not being top sellers by volume.
2. Regional Trends
o The **USA** accounted for the highest number of orders **23%** to the total sales, making it the top-performing market.
o **Canada** recorded the lowest performance, showing a need for market repositioning strategies.
3. Sales Trends Over Time
o Sales peaked in April, followed by a gradual decline starting in June.
o A minor dip occurred in February, though less significant compared to the later decline.
## Recommendations
1. Boost Underperforming Products: Re-market Charcoal Face Wash through promotions or bundling, to improve sales.
2. Expand Regional Market: Strengthen distribution and targeted campaigns in Canada, to capture market share while strengthening loyalty programs in USA.
3. Seasonality Strategy: Analyze factors behind the April sales spike and prepare campaigns to counter the June decline.
4. Data Enrichment: Collect customer demographics, campaign tracking, and seasonal demand factors for deeper insight.
